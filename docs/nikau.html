About

My work plays off the social and creative potential of digital realms, exploring them as ideal spaces for utopian reimaginings of society. Through playful and introspective audience participation, we together create an archive of individual and collective utopias. By collating shared individual and collective longings for a safe, secure, and sustainable future, my work is an optimistic attempt to speak this future into existence, examining utopia as the culmination, source, and product of desire.
